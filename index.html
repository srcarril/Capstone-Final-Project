About

Our Project

Journey of the Soul is a web-based narrative focused project that gives today's youths the mindfulness tools and techniques to conquer their own challenges in life and to encourage emotional self-regulation in pre-teens.

About our Work

Behind The Scenes

Goals

Our goal for this project was to create an accessible and reachable resource able to reach our youths in modern educational and familial environments, while also incorporating sound and visuals to tell a contemporary fable capable of providing the ability to teach pre-teens mindfulness and self-regulation techniques for them to use intentionally for themselves.
‍

Audience

The targeted young readers are
pre-adolescents from the ages 9-13 years old.
(open to any reader able to read text)
‍
We specifically chose this audience based on the research and study of children we learned at this age. During this period of time, the young mind is struggling through many changes including emotional, physical, and cognitive developments.

Methods

Code: Web-based html, Java, CSS, Vanilla Javascript
‍
Art: Procreate, Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects
‍
Sound: FL Studio, Reaper

Our Motivation


Having spent a great deal of time in isolation, this led to an excessive increase in anxiety, depression and stress seen in pre-adolescent and adolescent youths today according to Andrea Hussong, professor and associate director of clinical psychology in the department of psychology and neuroscience in UNC.  

People living in this pandemic age have turned to yoga, meditation, and other methods to cope with their negative emotions and stress. Learning these techniques as an adult is difficult because they are often distracted by their past or concerned by their future, whereas children are similar to sponges and always ready to learn, so when these mindfulness techniques are developed in youth it is easier for them to soak into their experiences. 

So in our fable, we incorporated mindfulness techniques: External Conflict Resolution, Internal Conflict Resolution: Accountability in the face of Anxiety, and Body Scan meditation: Emotional self-regulation.

After enduring through the pandemic, our team, and hopefully society has recognized the importance of mental health and the need to promote mindfulness not only in adults but children as well. We hope our project and the tools we introduce to the youths of today will make it into the next generation that has these tools at their disposal and to use them to their advantages.
